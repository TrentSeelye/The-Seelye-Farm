About The Seelye Farm

The Seelye Farm is a small, family‑run operation committed to raising poultry and eggs the right way—on pasture, in fresh air, and with daily hands‑on care. Our mission is simple: produce high‑quality food while improving the land we steward.

We believe healthy land creates healthy birds—and healthy birds create exceptional food.

Our Regenerative Approach

Regenerative farming is at the heart of everything we do. Instead of confining birds indoors, we raise them on open pasture and move them frequently to protect the soil, encourage natural foraging, and keep the flock clean and healthy.

Daily movement for meat birds

Our meat birds are moved every single day to a fresh section of pasture. This prevents overgrazing, spreads nutrients evenly, and ensures the birds always have access to clean grass, insects, and sunlight.

Weekly movement for our laying coop

Our laying hens live in a mobile coop that is rotated weekly across the pasture. This keeps the ground healthy, reduces parasite pressure, and allows the hens to scratch, forage, and fertilize the land naturally.

Why These Practices Matter

Regenerative methods take more time and effort, but the results speak for themselves. By working with nature instead of against it, we produce food that is better for the land, better for the birds, and better for you.

  • Cleaner, healthier birds: Frequent movement keeps bedding fresh, reduces disease risk, and eliminates the need for confinement‑style systems.
  • Superior flavor and nutrition: Birds that forage on grass and insects produce richer, more nutrient‑dense meat and eggs with deep golden yolks.
  • Regenerated soil: Manure is spread evenly across the pasture, feeding the soil instead of overwhelming it in one place.
  • Reduced feed waste: Birds naturally supplement their diet with forage, which improves their health and reduces reliance on grain.
  • Environmentally responsible: Rotational movement prevents bare patches, erosion, and compaction—keeping the land vibrant and productive.
  • Ethical animal care: Our birds live outdoors with space to roam, scratch, dust bathe, and behave like birds should.
Customers often tell us they can taste the difference—and we believe that comes from the care we put into every step of the process.

Our Promise

When you buy from The Seelye Farm, you’re supporting a farm that prioritizes transparency, land stewardship, and animal welfare. We’re proud to raise food you can feel good about feeding your family.
